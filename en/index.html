I am a civil engineer specializing in hydraulics and sanitation, with over 12 years of experience designing, modeling, and supervising water infrastructure projects in Colombia, Ecuador, Peru, and more than a dozen additional countries.

My approach combines the technical rigor of numerical modeling with digital twin innovation, enabling anticipation of flood scenarios, optimization of distribution networks, and design of efficient treatment systems under sustainability and climate resilience criteria.

I collaborate with local governments, consulting firms, and multilateral organizations (IDB, World Bank, CAF) to develop solutions that positively impact the quality of life of urban and rural communities.
